My MSIT Journey

About Me:

I am Archana Vellanki. I am from Anantapur, Andhra Pradesh and I have done my schooling and intermediate from the same place. I have done my BTech from JNTU College of Engineering, Anantapur. Post my btech I worked for 2 years at Accenture. I came to know about this course through my relatives and attempted the entrance exam in which I secured 3rd rank. It was my mom and sister who inspired me to join this course.

MSIT is completely different from other traditional academic courses. It targets for making the individuals ready for the industry which makes them very much employable. Some of the unique features of MSIT are as follows:

Unique Features Value adding
Learning by doing This is the heart of MSIT curriculum. Helps the students to understand the concepts better by having hands on experience.
Project based curriculum Simultaneous projects help them to learn and apply the theoretical knowledge in a better way.
Mini Class Rooms The class rooms are compact with just 12 - 15 members per each. This helps the students and mentors to be more involved and focused.
Dynamic Rules This enables MSIT to dynamically modify the way of designing the courses as per the industrial requirement.

MSIT selectively picks the courses based on booming technologies. This enables students to learn what is trending in the industry and makes them industry ready professionals. The courses are well designed as per project based curriculum.

  • Essential Relationship Building
